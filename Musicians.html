Our Artists

Issce Gonzalez

Issce Gonzalez is a jazz trumpeter from Dallas, Texas . His musical journey started in middle school, when he got his first trumpet. Issce has lead, performed, and received multiple awards throughout his younger educational years in different ensembles performing classical music. In high school, Issce was the lead trumpeter in the Symphonic band and traveled throughout Texas winning competitions. After High School he was given a scholarship to go to the University of Arkansas where he attended for two years before going to Los Angeles.

In that time, Issce has performed in many groups before making his own, forming his group with bassist Enrique Duarte IV. Together they’ve performed all throughout Los Angeles, and Hollywood. You can find Issce and Enrique performing throughout Beverly Hills, bringing a cool nuanced vibe to jazz. Interpreting all the great trumpeters such as, Roy Hargrove, Freddie Hubbard, Miles Davis, Donald Byrd, and Woody Shaw. Issce’s hope is to follow in their example and to continue the lineage of the great trumpeters. To bring back an elegance and sophistication to the genre. Ultimately, “the objective is mastery”
