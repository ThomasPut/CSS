What's Nothing But Jazz?

Nothing but jazz is an emerging record label and brand dedicated to promoting and perpetuating the art form of jazz.

Founded in 2022, by Ted Dozier, with the inspiration being Ice Gonzalez and Enrique Duarte IV. The trio came together with the dedication mission to both preserve and promote the rich heritage of jazz.

With a team of dedicated producers, engineers, and creative professionals, the label excels in delivering extraordinary live Jazz performances.
